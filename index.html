About us

Pizza.co has been creating over 40 years of great times and great memories in the Philippines. Having started its first store in Metro Manila way back in 1975, Pizza.co now operates nationwide with a strong store count of 307 stores, comprising of 171 Company-owned stores and 136 franchised stores. Pizza.co is one of the leading operators of Fast Casual Restaurants in the Philippines, focused on family casual dining. In 2020, Euromonitor reported Pizza.co as the market leader in both chained pizza full-service restaurant and chained full-service restaurant.

A better pizza begins from the bottom up. That’s why the dough used for our hand-tossed, original crust is prepared with the finest ingredients.

Pizza.co USA launched online pizza ordering in 2007, and in 2008 revolutionized the online delivery experience by launching the innovative Pizza Builder and Pizza.co Tracker tools.

A major part of the recipe is Pizza.co deliciously fresh and irresistibly tasty topping ingredients that we scatter generously onto our famously fresh dough bases!
